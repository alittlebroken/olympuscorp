Company Values

Olympus Corp is THE leading innovator of Space Colonisation & Exploration and all associated Sciences since it's inception in 1981.

Here at Olympus Corp we aim to ensure humanities future among the stars. Our core values are trust, loyalty and commitment to ensure our mission.

We truly are one big family here at Olympus Corp and each and every one of us makes an invaluable contribution to our success.

Missions

We pride ourselves on our past, present and future contribution to shape humanities future among the stars. The missions below are just a small peek at what has been accomplished by our company in a short amount of time.

OCSS-Apollo


An network of satellites orbit the sun harvesting its solar radiation and beam this back to processing and storage arrays stored on the moon.

Currently operating at 15% capacity

OCSS-Hermes


Satellites have been deployed throughout our solar system up to the orbital plane of Jupiter.

This affords near realtime communication from anywhere this side of Jupiter.

OCSS-Demeter


We have deployed this state of the art telescope on the far side of the moon to aid the finding of resource rich objects in and outside of our solar system.

OCSS-Artemis


A complementary mission to SolSearch in that when a viable resource rich object is found a probe is deployed to tag for future mining missions, and provides a detailed map of the resources contained within or on the object we are interested in.

OCSS-Zeuss


The flagship of our fleet is currently tasked with exploring all sectors of our solar system.

To push back the boundaries of the unknown and further enrich mankinds knowledge of and place in the universe.
